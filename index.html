Modern College (Approved by AICTE, New Delhi, DTE, Govt. of Maharashtra and Affiliated to the University of Mumbai) was established under the aegis of Late Shri Vishnu Waman Thakur Trust with the aim of imparting higher knowledge in Science and Technology to the aspiring boys and girls so that they can emerge as competent scientists, engineers and technologists of repute. Modern College was started in the year 2009. We have fully equipped laboratories in all the departments. To support the knowledge needs of the students, we have fully computerized library with more than 11110 books, international journals and e-journals and several national journals.
